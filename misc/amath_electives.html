Applied Math Major - "Related Fields"
The following courses have been APPROVED to count as electives for the Applied Math Major
  • All Math courses numbered 61 or above.
  • Chemistry (CHEM) 133
  • Computer Science (COMP) 160, 170
  • Economics (EC) 108, 118, 119, 154, 201, 202
  • Electrical and Computer Engineering (EE) 18, 107, 108, 125
  • Engineering Science (ES) 152
  • Mechanical Engineering (ME) 137, 150, 166
  • Philosophy (PHIL) 103, 170
  • Physics (PHY) 12, 13, and any course numbered 31 or above
  • Psychology (PSY) 140
The following courses will NOT be allowed to count as an elective for the Applied Math Major
  • Biology (BIO) 133
  • Computer Science (COMP) 11, 15
  • Economics (EC) 107, 169, 191
  • Philosophy (PHIL) 33, 114
  • Psychology (PSY) 107, 108
  • Sociology (SOC) 101
Students who would like to request approval of a different 'related fields' course (not mentioned above) to count as an elective should send, in electronic form, a detailed description of the course they would like to be approved to applmathrelfieldapproval [at] tufts [dot] edu.
