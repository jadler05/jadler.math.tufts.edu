Applied Math Seminar E-List Instructions


This page contains instructions for signing up for the general Applied Math Seminar e-list.

Anyone on this mailing list can use it for making announcements about applied math seminars that are taking place throughout the year.
If you are interested in getting these announcements, you can easily subscribe to this list in one of two ways:
  • Either go to the elist webpage:

    https://elist.tufts.edu/wws/info/appliedmath-seminar

    and click Subscribe on the bottom left. You may need to enter your Tufts UTLN and password to log in.

  • Or you can simply email: sympa@elist.tufts.edu
    In the subject, enter the following:

    sub appliedmath-seminar

    You can leave the body blank. This way, I believe, you can use any email you wish to receive the announcements.
Alternatively, you can email me directly to have you added.
There will be a short approval period before you are actually subscribed.

Also, if you know of anyone else that would be interested in being on this list please forward them this information on how to subscribe.
